The Way We Connect Clients and Workers:

1. Client Posting a Job:

  • A client initiates the process by posting a job.
  • The job post is then stored in the system's database.

2. Job Visibility to Workers:

  • The posted job becomes visible to workers who are registered on the platform.
  • Workers can browse through the available jobs and search for ones that match their skills and preferences.

3. Worker Applying for a Job:

  • A worker interested in a job can apply for it.
  • The application is recorded in the database.

4. Client Reviewing Applications:

  • The client can review the applications received for their job.
  • They can assess the workers' profiles and choose the most suitable candidate.

5. Job Assignment to Worker:

  • Once a client selects a worker, the job is assigned to that worker.
  • This information is updated in the database.

6. Worker Accessing Job Details:

  • The assigned worker gains access to the specific details of the job.
  • This includes the job description, location, and any other relevant information.

7. Worker Completing the Job:

  • The worker completes the job as per the agreed terms.

8. Client Feedback and Payment:

  • The client provides feedback on the worker's performance.
  • Payment is made to the worker for the completed job.

Key Components:

  • Client: The person or entity seeking to hire a worker for a specific task.
  • Worker: The individual who performs the tasks requested by the client.
  • Job Post: The information about the job, including the task, location, and requirements.
  • Workers List: A list of available workers who can be hired for jobs.
  • Handy Help: The platform that facilitates the connection between clients and workers.
  • Client Dashboard: The interface where clients can post jobs, review applications, and manage their tasks.
  • Worker Dashboard: The interface where workers can view available jobs, apply for tasks, and manage their work.
  • Database: The system that stores information about jobs, workers, and their interactions.

In essence, Handy Help provides a platform for clients and workers to efficiently connect and collaborate on various tasks, streamlining the process for both parties.
